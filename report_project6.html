Task 2: Hyperstreamlines

The locations of the seeding was hard to find, since most of the locations will either create a very short Hyperstreamline or cover a short region of the brain. For this task I was trying to cover most of the brain region. In order to make this, I took the center of the DTI data and used this point as a starting position for the streamlines.

Final results

Although I was able to obtain a large area covered by the the streamlines, I think that this method alone does not tell a lot onf information an localization of the streamlines. The user will not be able to locate where the streamlines are with respect to the other regions of the brain.

Task 3: Volume Rendering and Fibers

Creating the transfer function

I wanted that the transfer function will be able to separate the streamlines from the brain. to achieve this I used a color scale where the region that is not used in the lines is in a opposed color. this method allow the used to properly identify where the HyperStreamlines are located in contrast with the other regions of the brain.

Results

I find this approach much better that the one completed in task 2. It gives the user a sense in where the hyperstreamlines are located inside the brain.
